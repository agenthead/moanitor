FAQ

Common questions

Will it moan during a meeting?
Nope! Our Smart Silence technology detects when your microphone is in use and mutes itself automatically. You stay professional, we stay silent.
Does it work without admin?
Yes, but hardware temperature reading requires admin privileges on most systems.
Is it a virus?
No, it's just a lonely piece of software that wants to feel connected to your hardware.
Can I add my own sounds?
Not yet, but we're working on it! Your support helps us get there faster.

Let's keep the moans free.

Moanitor is a passion project built for the vibes. We don't believe in subscriptions or paywalls. Your support on Ko-fi helps cover costs and fuels the development of new features like Multi-Character Logic and Custom Hotkeys.
